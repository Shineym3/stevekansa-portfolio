About
A little about me

I'm a product design leader based in Atlanta (fully remote). I specialize in turning ambiguous, complex problems into clear, usable products — with a bias toward shipping and learning fast.

I've led design at companies ranging from pre-seed startups to large enterprises, always operating at the intersection of user research, systems thinking, and hands-on craft. I believe great design is both a strategic asset and a daily practice.

When I'm not designing, I'm thinking about how AI is reshaping the designer's role — and making sure I'm ahead of it, not behind it.
